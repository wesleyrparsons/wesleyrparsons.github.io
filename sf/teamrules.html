South Florida Elite Tournament Rules

  • Bring a birth certificate to each tournament.
  • All players are required to stay at the same hotel.
  • Hotel arrangements for the players will be made by the coaches.
  • All players are required to eat together.
  • Parents staying with players pay their own hotel expenses.
  • Hotel prices will usually be in the range of $60 to $75 per night.
  • Players traveling without parent stay 3 to 4 to a room, and divide the hotel expenses.
  • Parents and players pay their own meal expenses.
  • Curfews will be established when staying in hotels.
