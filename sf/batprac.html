Optional JV Batting Practice

Monday, May 24, 6 to 8 p.m.

Event

The JV Elite will hold an optional batting practice on Monday, May 24, from 6 to 8 p.m. Players (and parents if they wish) can show up at any time between 6 and 8 p.m, and stay as long as they wish.

Location

The batting practice will at Wes Parsons' house, 9838 S.W. 106 Terrace, Miami. There is a 55-foot batting cage and a Jugs Iron Mike pitching machine, which can throw up to 90 m.p.h. One player can use the cage at a time.

To find 9838 S.W. 106 Terrace, get on 97th Avenue from Kendall Drive, Killian Drive, 128th Street, Howard Drive, or 144th Street. 106th Terrace is on the west side of 97th Avenue, across the street from Killian Senior High School. Number 9838 is on the left near the end of the street (which makes a U-curve and continues as 108th Terrace and connects back to 97th Avenue.) Please park on the driveway or grass at 9838 S.W. 106 Terrace--but not on the neighbors' yards.

Batting Cage Rules

Attendance is optional. Please come if you want some extra batting practice. By attending, the players and their parents agree to the following batting cage rules: Always wear a helmet. Only one person in the cage at a time. No horseplay in or near cage. Look for the red light on the machine--it means a pitch is coming. Be aware a pitch can be thrown for a few seconds after the machine powers down.
