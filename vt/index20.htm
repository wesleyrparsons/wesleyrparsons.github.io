Die Deutsche Volkstanz und Trachten Gruppe

The German-American Social Club of Greater Miami




Our dance group performs traditional folk dances from the various regions of Germany, Austria and Switzerland, keeping our heritage and traditions alive in the new world. We specialize in Polkas, Waltzes, Rheinländer, and Mazurkas.

Die Deutsche Volkstanz und Trachten Gruppe was founded in 1976 under the direction of Bill Woessner, a native German from Schwenningen in the Black Forest.

We are members of the German-American Social Club of Greater Miami, and dance at the GASC clubhouse, located at 11919 S.W. 56th Street, Miami, FL 33175. We practice Sundays at 3:30 p.m. Newcomers are welcome. Call Elke Kraushaar, the current dance group leader, at 305-274-3782.
